ATMS Extended Features and Information

The Advanced Traffic Management Service (ATMS) is a comprehensive traffic management system that goes beyond basic traffic monitoring and control. It is a sophisticated system that utilizes advanced technologies to gather and analyze various data to provide valuable insights for traffic management and control. In addition to the features already mentioned, such as vehicle counting, accident counting, and weather reports, the ATMS can offer several other types of information to support traffic management efforts.

Predictive Analytics

The ATMS can use historical and real-time data to predict traffic patterns and trends. This information can be used to anticipate congestion and proactively implement measures to alleviate it, such as adjusting signal timings or rerouting traffic. Predictive analytics can also help in planning road maintenance activities and optimizing road network capacity.
