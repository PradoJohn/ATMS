Predictive Analytics

The ATMS can use historical and real-time data to predict traffic patterns and trends. This information can be used to anticipate congestion and proactively implement measures to alleviate it, such as adjusting signal timings or rerouting traffic. Predictive analytics can also help in planning road maintenance activities and optimizing road network capacity.
