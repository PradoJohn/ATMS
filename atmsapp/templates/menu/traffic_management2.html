GPS Sensor Coloration Indicator

The GPS sensors receive data parameters including Longitude, Latitude, and Speed. The system then processes the longitude and latitude to determine the accurate location of the GPS sensors. Based on the location of the GPS sensor, the system identifies the type of road it is on. Once the road type is determined, the system processes the information to approximate the traffic flow gathered by the GPS sensor. Depending on the traffic flow, the system identifies the following coloration:
